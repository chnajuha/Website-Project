What are these courses about?

Basic Computer Guide Course

The Basic Computer Guide Course are suitable for people who are interested and want to learn the basic use of the computer. For example, learning to write an email.

Cyber Security Course

Today, there are increased number of cyber crime which includes hacktivism, cyber ransoms and other incidents that may potentially bring down an organisation. This course will allow people to have the necessary skills to prevent such danger from happening.

Virus Protection Course

Is your device always slow? Or is it always rebooting by itself? Or is it always not working the way your device should be working? No problem! The Virus Protection Course allows people to learn how to protect your devices from virus threats.

Trouble Shooting Course

Can't seem to find the solution to your problem on your device? The Trouble Shooting Course will be the solution to your problem. The course will teach people on how to identify problems and solve it on the spot.
